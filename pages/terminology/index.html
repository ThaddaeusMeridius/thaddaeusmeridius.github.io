Style Guide

Consistency is key, and I will do my best to keep everything on this site consistent across all topics, in regard to mathematical notation. Below is how I will be presenting that notation.

All vectors will be bold and have an arrow above them $$\vec{\mathbf{F}}, \vec{\mathbf{a}}$$ All matrices will be capital letters in either the roman or greek alphabet $$\Psi, A$$ Real or Euclidean space will have the same notation in blackboard, unless needed to emphasize its Euclidean nature $$\mathbb{R}$$ Complex number space will be denoted as $$\mathbb{C}$$ Natural numbers will be denoted as $$\mathbb{N}$$ Quaternions/Hamiltonian Quaternions will be denoted as $$\mathbb{H}$$ Projective space $$\mathbb{P}$$ Rational numbers $$\mathbb{Q}$$ Integers $$\mathbb{Z}$$ Approximately equal to $$\cong$$ Equivalently equal to $$\equiv$$ Implies $$\implies$$ Proportional to $$\propto$$ Union $$\cup$$ Intersect $$\cap$$ Cross-product $$\times$$ Empty set $$\varnothing$$
